# Online Retail II – Sales Analysis

This project explores the Online Retail II dataset to understand sales patterns, customer behavior, and best-selling products.  
All analysis is done in Python using Pandas and Matplotlib within a Jupyter Notebook.

---

## Key Insights

### Top products by quantity
The products with the highest sales volume are:

→ White Hanging Heart T-Light Holder — 57,733 units  
→ World War 2 Gliders Assorted Designs — 54,698 units  
→ Brocade Ring Purse — 47,647 units

### Top products by revenue
Based on `TotalPrice = Quantity × Price`, the most profitable products are:

→ Regency Cakestand 3 Tier — $163,051  
→ White Hanging Heart T-Light Holder — $157,865  
→ Dotcom Postage — $116,401

### Sales by country
The United Kingdom leads in total sales, followed by:

→ Ireland  
→ Netherlands  
→ Germany  
→ France

### Monthly sales trends
Sales show seasonal variation, peaking in:

→ October 2010  
→ November 2010 (highest revenue month)  

December shows a drop, likely due to partial data.
